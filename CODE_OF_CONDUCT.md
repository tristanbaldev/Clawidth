# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

Clawidth is a welcoming project. Everyone is welcome to contribute regardless of
experience level, background, or identity. We're here to build something fun and
to help each other do it.

## Be excellent to each other

- Be respectful, patient, and constructive — especially with beginners. This is
  a learn-to-build project, and everyone started somewhere.
- Assume good intent. Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Keep discussion on-topic and welcoming.

## Not okay

Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, personal attacks, trolling, publishing
others' private information, or any conduct that makes the community feel unsafe.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and
issues that violate this code, and may ban repeat or serious offenders.

To report a problem, contact the maintainer privately via GitHub
([@tristanbaldev](https://github.com/tristanbaldev)).
